Our Story

Vibe Coders was founded by a group of passionate students who wanted to make coding accessible, fun, and collaborative for everyone. What started as a small after-school club quickly grew into a vibrant community of learners, creators, and innovators.

We believe in the power of teamwork, creativity, and sharing knowledge. Our workshops are designed to inspire, challenge, and empower high schoolers to build real-world projects and develop lifelong skills.
